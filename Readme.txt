Super Space Wars

The galaxy is at war! How long can you survive?
Shot, dodge and collect items to help you.


Game made using Godot Engine 3.1 for GBJAM 8
Theme: A game inspired from Gameboy console.


CONTROLS:
W/S - Move forward/backward.
A/S - Rotate left/right.
J - Shoot

*On title screen, maybe you need to click one of the options,
Use W/S to go up or down the menu and ENTER to choose the option.
